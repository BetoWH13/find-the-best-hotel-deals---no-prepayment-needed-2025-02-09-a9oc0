About Us

Today, we're committed to revolutionizing the hotel booking experience by eliminating upfront payments and providing transparent, customer-first services. Frequently Asked Questions

How does the no-prepayment system work?

You can book your hotel room without any upfront payment. Simply secure your reservation with your credit card details, and pay the full amount directly at the hotel during check-in.

What is your cancellation policy?

Most bookings can be cancelled free of charge up to 24-48 hours before check-in. Specific policies vary by property and are clearly displayed during booking.

Are your rates the same as other booking sites?

Yes, we offer competitive rates comparable to other major booking platforms, with the added benefit of no upfront payment required.

What payment methods are accepted at check-in?

Most hotels accept major credit cards, debit cards, and cash. Specific payment options are listed in the hotel details during booking.

Is my booking guaranteed without prepayment?

Yes, your booking is 100% guaranteed. We secure your reservation with the hotel directly, ensuring your room will be ready upon arrival.
